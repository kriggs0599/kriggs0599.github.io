About Us

Kenny Riggs is the Technology Integration Specialist in the Fab Lab at Belen Jesuit Preparatory School. He teaches both introductory and honors-level computer science courses using the PLTW curriculum. Beyond the classroom, Kenny leads innovation in digital learning and helps shape a forward-thinking STEAM environment for students.

Future Coders Initiative is a nonprofit dedicated to bridging the digital divide by providing access to STEM education, IT certifications, and mentorship for underrepresented youth in inner-city communities.

Programs

  • After-school Tutoring & Literacy Labs
  • Educational Summer Camps
  • STEM Competitions & Makerspace Workshops
  • IT Certifications through Cisco Networking Academy
  • Girls Who Code & Robotics Clubs
